Your companion in creating a safer online world for your children.

What is Think Twice?

Think Twice is a browser extension and dashboard that helps parents of children aged 11 to 14 detect and understand toxic or harmful content online. Whether it’s a suspicious comment, a bullying message, or a concerning trend, Think Twice highlights it and gives you insights in real-time.

Key Features

  • 🔍 Analyze websites for signs of toxicity or bullying.
  • 📊 View summary reports on a clean dashboard.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Designed with parents and children in mind.
  • 🧩 Easy to install browser extension.
